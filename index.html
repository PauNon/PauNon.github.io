About Me

Design with clarity,
creativity, and heart.

I design visuals that make learning easier — and actually enjoyable. From graphic design and illustration to character design and interactive modules, I bring ideas to life through tools I've spent years mastering.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me playing video games, spending time with my dogs (and admiring other people's dogs), or treating food like the art form it truly is.

That mix of curiosity, playfulness, and attention to detail shows up in everything I create. I care deeply about how things feel, not just how they look.
